Player Controls

  • a and d — translate view left and right along view X
  • w and s — translate view forward and backward along view Z
  • q and e — translate view up and down along view Y
  • shift + a or d — rotate view left and right around view Y (yaw)

    Game Controls

  • left and right — change position left and right
  • up and down — change position up and down
  • PgUp and PgDn — change position forward and backward
  • space — spawn pyramid at index (game automatically changes players)
  • r - reset game state
